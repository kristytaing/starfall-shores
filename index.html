✿ Starfall Shores ✿

An Island Adventure

HOW TO PLAY

🚶 WASD or Arrow Keys to move
💬 E or Space to talk & interact
🎒 I to open inventory
📜 Q to view quest log
🔊 M to toggle sound

🌟 Explore the island, befriend the locals, and collect 5 Starfall Crystals to save the island!
📱 Mobile: virtual joystick + ACT/BAG buttons
Click the button to begin
▼ Press Space

🎒 Inventory

Press I or tap to close

📜 Quest Log

Press Q or tap to close

🗺️ Island Map

Click a location to fast-travel there

Press M or tap to close
